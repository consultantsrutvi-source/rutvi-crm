How to use — Quick Guide

  1. Open Requirements, fill college info → Save. Check "Requirements" sheet in Google Sheets for saved row.
  2. Open Lecturers, add candidates (email optional) → Save. Check "Lecturers" sheet.
  3. Open Placements, enter joining & first salary date, set consultancy % and GST. Click Generate Bill → Preview invoice → Save.
  4. After saving Placement, EMI calendar links become available; click Add EMI dates to Google Calendar to open event pages (save them in your Google Calendar).
  5. Use Summaries to print table-style summaries. Use Print buttons to save PDFs for record or print on letterhead.
  6. If rows are not being saved: ensure Apps Script is deployed (Execute as: Me; Who has access: Anyone) and replace the SCRIPT_URL in this file with your current webapp /exec URL.
Notes: The page uses your Apps Script URL (JSONP). Do not change the script URL unless you redeploy. If you redeploy Apps Script, update the SCRIPT_URL here and republish.
